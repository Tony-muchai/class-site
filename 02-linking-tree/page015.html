Luanda was an invinsible warrior with a body of stone which could withstand the brutality of battle. He belonged to the Luo community in East Africa a bitter rival to the Nandi tribe. Determined to defeat Luanda and gain dominance they sent a spy to marry him and learn his weakness in battle. Unable to resist the beautyful Nandi girl, Luanda fell into their trap. With time, she learnt that his weakness lay in his shadow, that if anyone harmed it, his body would feel the pain. This knowlwedge proved to be deadly. His wife betrayed him and his next battle was his last!
